What the app does

Money Tracker: Income Month is a personal finance calculator. The app stores all values you enter locally on your device. There is no account creation, no cloud sync, no analytics, and no advertising SDKs.

Information we collect

We do not collect personal data from the app. If you contact support by email, we will receive your email address and message solely to reply to you. This is not linked to your in-app activity and is not used for tracking or marketing.

Data storage & retention

Finance data remains on your device. Support emails are kept only as long as needed to handle your request and then deleted.

Sharing & selling

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.

Permissions

The app does not request device permissions (camera, photos, location, contacts, notifications).

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your rights

You can request deletion of support correspondence or ask privacy questions at barapp.design@gmail.com.

Changes

Updates to this policy will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
